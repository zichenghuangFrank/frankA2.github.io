Calculator structure and instructions: 
1. All numbers (0-9) and the decimal point (.)
2. Basic arithmetic operators(+,-,* and /)
3. The backspace (CE), delete the last digit 
4. The all clear (AC) and equal sign (=)
5. Left and right brackets ('(' and ')')
6. If the expression or equation is invalid, "Error" will display on the display bar
7. After one calculation, use AC to clear all or enter a number to start a new calculation
8. The result will show on the display bar and the ans (previous answer) will show on the display ans bar
9. If you want to use the previous answer, click the operator button for the next calculation
